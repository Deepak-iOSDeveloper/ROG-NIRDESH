About ROGNIRDESH

Rog = Illness  ·  Nirdesh = Guidance

ROGNIRDESH is an AI-powered clinical decision support platform designed for Indian healthcare providers. It bridges language barriers, surfaces recovery insights, and groups patients intelligently — so doctors can focus on what matters most: patient care.

What We Offer

Four pillars powering smarter healthcare

🌐
Multilingual Symptom Translation

AI translates patient symptoms across 5 Indian languages — Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada — into clear, structured clinical English for accurate diagnosis.

📈
Recovery Trend Analysis

A custom linear regression ML model tracks patient health scores over time, detecting improvement or worsening trends and providing doctors with actionable recovery forecasts.

🧬
Smart Patient Clustering

An unsupervised K-Means algorithm groups patients by symptom similarity, enabling doctors to identify common patterns, manage outbreaks, and streamline population-level analysis.

👨‍⚕️
Doctor-Centric Workflow

Complete patient management — profiles, case tracking, periodic check-ins, and full data editing. Every tool a doctor needs, all in one streamlined interface.

How It Works

A streamlined workflow from intake to insight

1
Doctor Registers

Sign up with your phone number and medical license to access the platform.

2
Patient Intake

Enter patient details and describe symptoms in any supported Indian language.

3
AI Translation & Check-ins

Symptoms are auto-translated to clinical English. Add periodic check-ins to track progress.

4
Insights & Clustering

View recovery trend charts and smart patient clusters to guide treatment decisions.

Our Mission

To democratize clinical intelligence for healthcare providers across India, breaking language barriers and surfacing actionable insights through machine learning — so every patient gets the best possible care, regardless of which language they speak.
